Information Dissemination

Mar 30, 2018

This project aims to analyze the mechanisms of information propagation within social networks. What is the influence of the network topology? What is the influence of the nature of the message conveyed or its mode of transmission?

social-networks-analysis
Laurent Brisson
Associate Professor

Laurent BRISSON received a Ph.D degree in Computer Science from Université Nice Sophia-Antipolis (France) in 2006. His work focuses on the analysis of social networks along two axes: information dissemination and the detection of temporal communities.

Publications

Laurent Brisson, Philippe Collard, Martine Collard, Erick Stattner (2017). Information Dissemination in scale-free networks: Profusion versus Scarcity. International Conference on Complex Networks and Their Applications (COMPLEX NETWORK), Lyon, France.
